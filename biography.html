BIOGRAPHY




EARLY LIFE: Growing up, I was your typical kid who found joy in virtual adventures and the comfort of home.
While others explored the outdoors, my world revolved around the glow of a computer monitor.
Though some might find my childhood routine mundane, I found immense enjoyment in it.

EDUCATION: Throughout my elementary and high school years, I maintained a consistent record as an honor student.
Admittedly, I was more inclined to finish classes early and head home.
My high school journey led me from Colegio de San Bartolome to STI College Novaliches, where I initially pursued digital arts.
However, I later shifted to an IT course, realizing a new passion beyond drawing.

CAREER: Looking ahead, I see myself stepping into the professional realm as a developer,
with aspirations to eventually pursue a career in law, inspired by family members who have trod similar paths.

PERSONAL LIFE: Outside of academics and career aspirations, I'm a typical individual who cherishes moments spent with friends and values solitude.
Balancing social interactions with quiet moments brings me a sense of tranquility and fulfillment.

QUOTE: One quote that resonates deeply with me is from "The Godfather": "Men are not born great, they grow great."
It encapsulates the idea that greatness is earned through perseverance and resilience, rather than being inherent from birth.
